Who This Is For

We are building a community of African designers, researchers, and builders working on Bitcoin UX. The programme is in early development — this form puts you in the first cohort and helps us shape it around what members actually need.

There is no cost to join. We want people who care about building Bitcoin products that actually work for African users.

What Happens Next

We read every
submission personally.

This isn't an automated funnel. Every person who fills in this form gets read by a human. We'll reach out to everyone who submits — some sooner than others depending on how the community develops.

The community we're building will bring together African Bitcoin designers, UX researchers, product managers, and builders across Kenya, Nigeria, Ghana, South Africa, Ethiopia, and beyond. We're starting with the people closest to the research — and expanding from there.

In the meantime, you can read our published Bitcoin UX research at bitcoinux.africa, follow us on X @bitcoin_ux, or learn about how we run our research on the methodology page.
