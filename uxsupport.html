What We Do

Bitcoin UX Africa provides user research, usability testing, and design consulting for Bitcoin wallet teams targeting African markets. Our work is grounded in 340+ moderated user sessions across Kenya, Nigeria, Ghana, South Africa, and Ethiopia.

We know the exact moments where African users abandon Bitcoin wallets, and we know the design patterns that fix them. Wallet teams who implement our findings have reduced first-transaction failure rates and improved Day 30 retention significantly.
