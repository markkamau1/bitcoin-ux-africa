Privacy Policy

We collect as little as possible, never sell data, and are transparent about everything we do store.

Last updated: February 2026

Who We Are

Bitcoin UX Africa is an independent Bitcoin UX research organisation operating at bitcoinux.africa. Our contact email is mark@foundation.africa.

What Data We Collect

We collect the minimum data needed to operate this website:

  • Server logs: Standard web server logs including IP addresses, pages visited, browser type, and referrer URL. These are retained for 30 days and used solely for diagnosing technical issues.
  • Contact form submissions: If you submit a form on this site (via our UX Support or Join Community pages), we receive your name, email address, and message content. This data is processed by Formspree and subject to their privacy policy.
  • No cookies: This site does not set tracking cookies. We do not use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or any third-party tracking scripts.

What We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect payment information — we have no e-commerce on this site.
  • We do not track your behaviour across other websites.
  • We do not build advertising profiles.
  • We do not sell, rent, or trade your data to any third party under any circumstances.

Third-Party Services

This site uses the following third-party services:

  • Netlify — hosting and CDN. Subject to Netlify's privacy policy.
  • Google Fonts — fonts are loaded from Google's servers, which may log your IP. Subject to Google's privacy policy.
  • Formspree — contact form processing. Subject to Formspree's privacy policy.

Research Participant Data

Data collected during user research sessions (recordings, notes, observations) is handled under a separate research consent process explained to participants before any session begins. No participant is identified in published findings. Research recordings are stored securely and are never shared with third parties.

Your Rights

You have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of any personal data we hold about you. To make a request, email us at mark@foundation.africa. We will respond within 30 days.

Data Retention

Server logs are deleted after 30 days. Contact form submissions are retained until a response has been given, then deleted unless there is an ongoing business relationship. We do not retain data longer than necessary.

Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this policy, we will update the "last updated" date above. Continued use of this site after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
